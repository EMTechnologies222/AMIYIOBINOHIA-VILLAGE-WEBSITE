Help Us Tell Our Story

This website is a living document. Much of Amiyi Obinohia's history is preserved in oral tradition among elders and diaspora members. If you have knowledge, memories, photographs, or documents to contribute, please reach out.

We particularly welcome contributions on:

  • Family genealogies and histories
  • Historical photographs and documents
  • Traditional songs, proverbs, and stories
  • Records of community development projects
  • Names and stories of notable community figures
  • Information about traditional institutions and customs
  • Pre-colonial and early colonial era accounts

Find Amiyi Obinohia

Located in Isuikwuato LGA, Abia State, southeast Nigeria. Adjacent to Eluoma and near the Imo State boundary. Approximately 20 km from Umuahia.
